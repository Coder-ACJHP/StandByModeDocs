Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Data Collection and Usage

To improve app stability and enhance user experience, we utilize the following third-party services and technical methods:

  • Google Firebase Analytics: This service helps us understand user interactions (e.g., buttons clicked, screens visited) in an anonymized format. This data allows us to identify which features are most valuable to our users.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics: In the event of an unexpected crash or error, this service generates technical reports. These include device model, OS version, and stack traces to help us debug issues. This information does not personally identify you.
  • Technical Logging (OSLog): For performance monitoring and local debugging, the app may store diagnostic logs on your device. These logs remain on your device and are never transmitted to us unless explicitly provided by you for support purposes.

2. Identifiers

Our application may use anonymous device identifiers (such as IDFA for iOS or AAID for Android) solely for statistical analysis and error reporting purposes through Firebase services.

3. Third-Party Services

Data is processed using Google’s infrastructure. You can learn more about how Google handles your data by visiting:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security

4. Data Security

All collected data is stored on secure servers using industry-standard encryption. We do not sell your personal data or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.
